## Design Principles Applied

✓ **Choice within actions** - Each action offers 2-4 distinct strategic approaches
✓ **Clear progression** - Foundation → Leverage → Wealth path is obvious
✓ **Reduced cognitive load** - 12 visible cards vs 30 overwhelming options
✓ **Preserved depth** - Still 29 unique strategies, just organized better
✓ **Bundled related mechanics** - CFO hire handles payroll + tax + banking as one action
✓ **Flexible pathways** - Players can specialize (all marketing) or generalize (one of each)
